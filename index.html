Prewired++ Presents the Emporium of Digital Delights!

Explore digital creativity with a wide range of challenges, both easy and hard.

Work in a team during a two-week sprint to build something cool. Bring your friends or use the opportunity to meet new people who share your interests.

Open to any Edinburgh-based young people. You should be aged 12–18 and be fired with curiosity and enthusiasm. We value diversity and celebrate difference.
